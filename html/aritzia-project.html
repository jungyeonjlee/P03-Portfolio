Aritzia Exploration

MY ROLE

Research

Service Design

UI Design

TIMELINE

4 Week Undergrad Project

CONTEXT

Using industry precedent, I was in charge of researching an evidence-based rationale and executing for our design intervention for Aritzia.

PROBLEM

Customers found it difficult to start building a wardrobe that suits their wants and needs.

I initially recognized the disconnect between the customer and shopping experience which allowed me to frame the problem.

SOLUTION

Exploratory and Mission based shoppers.

What I aim to do with this iteration is to allow exploratory users to discover new items of clothing and feel confident about their choices due to the readily available information. However, there is also the choice for mission based customers that know what they are looking for to do so efficiently.
