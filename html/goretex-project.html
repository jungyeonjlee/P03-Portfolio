TIMELINE

4 Week Undergrad Project

CONTEXT

An undergrad design class that had us redesign a company website from the ground up.

Week 1

Our Initial Process and Style Guide.

Week 1 had us build out a a style guide with a consistent structure we can replicate in our real website down the line. We had to keep in mind an easy-to-read naming convention, clear type hierarchies, interaction points and its response.

Week 2

Building our Website.

Week 2 had us taking components of our style guide and finally applying them to our website. We made sure to link all of our pages together so we had a rough blueprint for our navigation. We slowly started finding high quality content to use and made sure to use semantically correct html.

Week 3

High Fidelity Prototype.

Week 3 was similar to the past week and finding more content and starting to slowly implement Javascript.

Week 4

Debugging Week.

Week 4 we ran into minor and major bugs that included margin and alignment errors. This week all of our content was finished, however we needed to polish up the entire website. This was all documented on our Git repository.
